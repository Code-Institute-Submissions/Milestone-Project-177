Barcelona, Spain

BARCELONA WEATHER

About Barcelona

Barcelona is situated on the North-Eastern coast of Spain. It is home to countless tourist attractions including one of the best beaches in the world and in the summer months has a beautifully warm climate making it perfect for summer holidays.
Barcelona is rich in culture, with over 55 museums, stunning gothic architecture and amazing Sports Venues.
Barcelona is also renowned for it's performing arts, with many venues for live music and theatre
Learn more about Barcelona

Things to do in Barcelona

With a wealth of culture and history, there is no lack of things to keep you busy in Barcelona. It is no surprise that Barcelona is Spain's most popular destination for international visitors.
See our top picks of things to do in Barcelona below:

Basilica of the Sagrada Familia

The Basilica of the Sagrada Familia is a large, unfinished Basilica (Roman Catholic Ceremonial church). It has been under construction since 1882 and is most well known for it's size and breath-taking beauty.

Book a tour
Casa Batllo

The Casa Batllo is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a staple of the City of Barcelona, welcoming around 1 million visitors each year. The vibrant colours and strangely designed architecture are just a few of the things which make the Casa Batllo such a tourist hot spot.

Book a tour
Camp Nou

The Camp Nou is home to the famous Barcelona Football team. It opened in 1957 and has a seating capacity of 99,354, making it the largest Stadium in Spain.
As well as hosting football matches, the Camp Nou often hosts major concerts.
